            <h1 class="title">Abstract</h1>
        </div>
        <div class="content">
            <div class="abstract-box">
                <p class="abstract-text">
                    This research enhances defect detection accuracy in wind turbine components through an <span class="highlight">ensemble of YOLO-based deep learning models</span> that integrate visible and thermal channels from UAV inspections. Our approach combines a general-purpose YOLOv8 model with a specialized thermal model using a sophisticated bounding box fusion algorithm, achieving a <span class="highlight">mean Average Precision (mAP@.5) of 0.93</span> and an F1-score of 0.90, outperforming standalone YOLOv8.
                </p>
            </div>
            <div class="key-points">
                <div class="point">
                    <i class="material-icons">flight</i>

                </div>
                <div class="point">
                    <i class="material-icons">layers</i>
                    <div class="point-title">Multispectral Fusion</div>
                    <div class="point-desc">Integration of RGB and thermal imagery for enhanced defect visibility</div>

                    <i class="material-icons">auto_awesome</i>
                    <div class="point-title">YOLO Ensemble</div>

                                </div>
                            </div>

                                <div class="step-number">2</div>
                                <div class="step-text">

                                </div>
                            </div>
                            <div class="algorithm-step">
                                <div class="step-number">3</div>
                                <div class="step-text">
                                    Create fused detection with combined information
                                </div>
                            </div>
                            <div class="algorithm-step">
                                <div class="step-number">4</div>
                                <div class="step-text">
                                    Preserve unique findings from each model
                                </div>
                            </div>
                            <div class="algorithm-step">
                                <div class="step-number">5</div>
                                <div class="step-text">

                        </div>

                    <div class="formula-box">
                        <div class="formula-title">Weighted Average Formulas</div>
                        <div class="formula">
                            p<sub>ensemble</sub> = γp<sub>Mt,j</sub> + (1−γ)p<sub>y,i</sub>
                        </div>
                        <div class="formula">
                            x<sub>ensemble</sub> = γx<sub>Mt,j</sub> + (1−γ)x<sub>y,i</sub>
                        </div>
                        <div class="formula">
                            y<sub>ensemble</sub> = γy<sub>Mt,j</sub> + (1−γ)y<sub>y,i</sub>
                        </div>

                            W<sub>ensemble</sub> = γW<sub>Mt,j</sub> + (1−γ)W<sub>y,i</sub>
